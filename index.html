Questions.

How is Dryft different from Mint, YNAB, or Copilot?

Those apps make you log, then report what already happened. Dryft tracks automatically and pings you in the moment, before you overspend.

I tried budgeting apps and quit. Why is Dryft different?

Because there's almost nothing to keep up with. No spreadsheets, no daily logging, no cell to break. The upkeep that made you quit is gone.

Will Dryft put me on a strict budget?

Never. No line items, no flat no. Just a gentle ping before an impulse buy, so you still live your life and reach your goals.

Do I have to track my spending with Dryft?

Almost never. Dryft tracks automatically via Plaid and watches the leaks for you. Now and then it may ask you to label a purchase, but there's no spreadsheet and no daily logging.

Can Dryft move my money?

No. Dryft has read-only access to your accounts via Plaid. It never holds your login and cannot move money.
