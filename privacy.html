1. Who we are

Dryft (“Dryft,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a finance-coaching product under active development, based in Ontario, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and handle personal information on dryft.app and through our waitlist communications.

Operating status. The Site is currently operated by Dryft's founder as a sole proprietorship. We expect to register a Canadian business in the coming weeks. Until that registration is complete, references to “Dryft,” “we,” “us,” or “our” refer to that founder; afterwards they will refer to the registered entity, and this Policy will be updated to reflect that.

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at muqeeth082005@gmail.com or by mail at the address in Section 13.

2. What we collect

On this website, we collect only what we need to operate the waitlist and improve the experience:

  • Email address — when you submit the waitlist form or the optional survey.
  • Survey responses (optional) — answers you choose to share about how you currently manage money.
  • Technical data — IP-derived approximate region, browser type, device type, referring site, and pages viewed. This is collected by our privacy-focused analytics provider (see Section 9) and is not tied to your email.

When the Dryft product launches, additional categories of personal and financial data will be collected under a separate, expanded privacy policy. We will notify you and obtain any required consent before that happens.

3. Why we collect it

  • To send you waitlist updates and a notification when Cohort 01 opens.
  • To prioritize survey takers for early access, as advertised.
  • To understand which messaging resonates and improve the site.
  • To prevent abuse (basic anti-spam and rate limiting).
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal bases are:

  • Consent — you give consent when you submit the waitlist form or the optional survey. You may withdraw it at any time (see Section 7).
  • Legitimate interest — for aggregated analytics, anti-abuse, and operating the site.

5. Service providers we share with

We share personal information only with the third parties needed to run the site and the waitlist:

  • Vercel — hosting and privacy-focused performance analytics. Vercel may process technical data on our behalf in the United States.
  • Google (Apps Script & Drive) — receives waitlist email submissions and stores them so we can contact you. Data is processed in the United States.
  • Plaid (future) — when the product launches, Plaid will provide read-only bank-transaction access. Plaid has its own privacy practices at plaid.com/legal.

We do not knowingly share personal information with any other third party, except where required by law (e.g. valid legal process) or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

6. How long we keep it

  • Waitlist email — until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until two (2) years after Cohort 01 closes if you never join the product.
  • Survey responses — for the duration of the waitlist plus two (2) years for product-research purposes, or until you request deletion.
  • Analytics data — aggregated and retained per Vercel's defaults (currently up to 12 months).

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information (the “right to be forgotten”).
  • Withdraw consent at any time — including unsubscribing from emails by replying “unsubscribe” or using the link in any commercial email.
  • Port a copy of your information to another service.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Complain to your data protection authority (e.g. the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, your EU/EEA supervisory authority, or the California AG).

To exercise any of these rights, email muqeeth082005@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days (sooner where required by law).

8. International transfers

Dryft is based in Canada. Our service providers process data in the United States and elsewhere. By using the site, you understand that your information may be transferred outside your country of residence, including to jurisdictions whose data-protection laws may differ from those in your country. We use contractual safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses, where applicable) to protect personal information during transfer.

9. Cookies & analytics

We do not use advertising cookies. We use Vercel's privacy-focused analytics, which counts page views and basic technical metrics without storing identifying cookies on your device. The site may set strictly-necessary cookies for security and session integrity. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (HTTPS, restricted access, encrypted storage where provided by our service providers). No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we ever learn of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

11. Minors

Dryft is intended for adults only. You must be at least 18 years of age (the age of majority in Ontario) to join the waitlist or use this Site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please email muqeeth082005@gmail.com and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices evolve or as required by law. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. For material changes, we will notify waitlist subscribers by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
